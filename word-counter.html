Frequently Asked Questions

How does the word counter work?
The tool splits your text by spaces and punctuation to count words accurately. Characters are counted including and excluding spaces. Sentences are detected by . ! ? punctuation.
How is reading time calculated?
Based on the average adult reading speed of 200 words per minute.
Is my text stored anywhere?
No. Everything runs in your browser. Your text is never sent to any server.
