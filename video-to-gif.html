Frequently Asked Questions

Is my video uploaded to a server?
No. Everything runs in your browser using the Canvas API and a JavaScript GIF encoder. Your video never leaves your device.
Why is the GIF file large?
GIF is an older format with limited color support (256 colors max) but no efficient video compression. For smaller files, use fewer fps, lower width, or shorter duration.
Which video formats are supported?
Any format your browser can play: MP4 (H.264), WebM, and on some browsers MOV and AVI.
