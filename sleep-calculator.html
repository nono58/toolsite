🧠 Why 90-minute cycles?

Sleep occurs in cycles of roughly 90 minutes, each containing light sleep, deep sleep, and REM. Waking mid-cycle causes grogginess (sleep inertia). Waking at the end of a cycle feels natural.

💤 NREM 1
Light sleep, ~5 min
💤 NREM 2
Body temp drops, ~20 min
🔵 NREM 3
Deep sleep, most restorative
🌈 REM
Dreams, memory consolidation

Adults need 5–6 cycles (7.5–9 hours). Teens need 6–7 cycles.
