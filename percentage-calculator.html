Frequently Asked Questions

How do I calculate 20% of 150?
Use the first calculator: enter 20 in Percentage and 150 in Number. Result: 30. Formula: (20 ÷ 100) × 150 = 30.
How do I calculate percentage increase?
Use the third calculator. Formula: ((New − Original) ÷ Original) × 100. Example: from 80 to 100 = 25% increase.
What is 15% tip on $85?
Enter 15% of 85 in the first calculator. Result: $12.75. Total with tip: $97.75.
