FAQ

How are words counted?
Words are sequences of characters separated by whitespace. Hyphenated words (e.g. "well-known") count as one word.
Is my text saved anywhere?
No. Everything runs in your browser. Your text never leaves your device.
What reading speed is used?
200 words per minute (average adult reading speed). Speaking time uses 130 wpm.
