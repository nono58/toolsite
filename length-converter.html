Frequently Asked Questions

How many feet are in a meter?
1 meter = 3.28084 feet. Or more precisely, 1 meter = 3 feet 3.37 inches.
How many kilometers in a mile?
1 mile = 1.60934 kilometers. So 5 miles = 8.047 km.
What is 5 foot 10 in centimeters?
5 feet 10 inches = 70 inches = 177.8 cm. Enter 70 and select Inch to see all units.
