How Age Is Calculated

Age calculation is more complex than it seems due to varying month lengths and leap years. The standard method: subtract the birth year from the current year, then adjust if the birthday hasn't occurred yet this year.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does the calculator account for leap years?
Yes. The tool counts exact days including leap years, so your age in days is always accurate.
What if I was born on February 29?
Leap day birthdays (Feb 29) are handled correctly — on non-leap years, the birthday is treated as March 1 for age calculation purposes.
Can I calculate someone else's age?
Yes — just enter any past date of birth. The tool works for any historical date.
How many days until my next birthday?
The tool shows a countdown to your next birthday based on today's date.
