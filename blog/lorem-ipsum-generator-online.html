What Is Lorem Ipsum?

Lorem Ipsum is dummy text derived from a work by the Roman philosopher Cicero written in 45 BC. The passage was scrambled in the 1500s and has been used as typesetter filler text ever since. The standard passage begins: "Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit..."

Why Use Lorem Ipsum Instead of Real Text?

  • Focus on design: Readable content distracts reviewers from evaluating layout and typography
  • No language bias: Lorem Ipsum looks like words but means nothing, so designs work internationally
  • Consistent length: You control exactly how much text fills a space
  • Avoid placeholders like "test test test" which look unpolished in mockups
